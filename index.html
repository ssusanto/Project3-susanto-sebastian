About Me

Meticulous, organized and curious; these are the qualities that define me. Attending The Kelley School of Business allows me to be challenged academically as well as individually. I am academically competitive in Kelley School of Business, and this can be proven based on the honors membership offered to me based on GPA. But excelling academically is not all I am worth.

During my internship at InterX Co. I had the chance to collect data by obtaining the different departments' excel files. I proceeded to analyze each of the departments' patterns and figured out a way to increase the efficiency on the manufacturing floor. After the analysis, the movement of the goods on the manufacturing floor is rerouted to promote efficiency. Through my previous example, it is evident that I like to acquire new knowledge about a wide variety of information. This shows that I value meticulousness and precision. This is a good character for someone who will be working with other people's finances. Aside from just that, I like to make informed decisions; this means that I do my due diligence before making an important decision. Most people believe that being overly meticulous, overly organized and overly curious is bad for you. I am not most other people. These are the qualities that make me unique.
